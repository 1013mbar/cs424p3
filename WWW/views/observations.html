Most of sightings are near the shores

 

Density of places created a solid shape, which showed that most of the sightings occur near shores. In the screenshot below, it shows only sightings of type "Lights".

Density

 

The County Population density graph shows that the number of reported sightings is directly proportional to the density population by county. This can be seen on the figure bellow.

Sightings by Year

 

Stacked bar graph shows that sightings were increasing up to 2008, and afterwards they began to decline. Interesting to note that proportions of each sighting remained similar.

Sightings by Time of the day

 

Time of the day graph showed that sightings occur mostly in the evening time. Interesting to note that there is a spike at 12pm (noon). Possibly could be due to input error due to pm/am confusion or due to the fact that people have their lunch breaks at the that time.

Increase in distance from airports leads to decrease in number of sightings

 

Graph below shows that number of sightings decline as distance from airport increases. This might suggest that people often confuse UFOs with men made flying objects.
